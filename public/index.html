How long does Red Alert stay active after enabling?
Once you enable Red Alert, it stays active until the moment you have set during the initial setup of your Red Alert Home Alarm. By default this is 08:00 in the morning.
When should I replace Red Alert?
Red Alert comes with a battery health overview, which shows you how healthy Red Alert is. When the battery health drops below 35% it will notify you via the display, advising you to either get a maintenance check or to buy a new one.
